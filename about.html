Short Biography

William W. Saul is an American music composer and nature enthusiast, primarily focused on electroacoustic pieces - often with found objects. Some of his recent subjects include wind-chimes, staircases, tree branches, and chainsaws.

During his formative years, William taught himself to produce EDM, DJ, play the piano, guitar, and write jazz, classical, and metal music. He also interned for multi-platinum producer Kevin Gutierrez. Currently, he is studying composition with Samuel Andreyev, and pursuing a B.A. at Berklee Online.

In his leisure hours, William spends his time outdoors with bow and arrows, being an avid archer for 12 years. He also enjoys traveling and playing chess.
