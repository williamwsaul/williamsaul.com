Short Biography

William W. Saul is an American music composer and nature enthusiast, primarily focused on electroacoustic pieces with found objects. Some of his recent subjects include whispers, staircases, tree branches, glass, and chainsaws.

During his formative years, William taught himself to produce EDM, DJ, play the piano, guitar, and write jazz, classical, and metal music. Currently, he is studying with esteemed composer Samuel Andreyev, interning for multi-platinum producer Kevin Gutierrez, and pursuing a B.A. in Music Composition for Film, Tv, and Games at Berklee Online.

In his leisure hours, William spends his time outdoors with bow and arrows, being an avid archer for 12 years. He also enjoys traveling and playing chess.
