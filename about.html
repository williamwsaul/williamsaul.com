William W. Saul is an electroacoustic composer and videographer.

Formatively, William studied with esteemed composer Samuel Andreyev, interned for multi-platinum producer Kevin Gutierrez, and earned a degree in media scoring remotely via Berklee Online. In addition to composing, he is also a self-taught pianist, guitarist, and intermedia artist.

In his leisure hours, William spends time outdoors with bow and arrows, having been an archer since age 11. He also enjoys traveling and playing chess.
