William W. Saul is an American music composer, artist, and nature enthusiast. Some of his primary focuses include electroacoustic and ‘modular’ composition, sound design using found objects, as well as videography.

During his formative years, William taught himself to play the piano and guitar, along with writing electronic, jazz, and classical music. He also studied with renowned composer Samuel Andreyev, and interned for multi-platinum producer Kevin Gutierrez. Currently, William is earning a B.A. remotely via Berklee Online.

In his leisure hours, William spends time outdoors with bow and arrows, being an archer since age 11. He also enjoys traveling and playing chess.
